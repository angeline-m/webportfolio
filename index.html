About

Hello, World! My name is Angeline and I'm a web developer. I truly believe I am destined for a career in digital media and development. The first sign was when I designed a personal homepage on MS Paint at 8 years old. These days, I love creating beautiful web applications using my programming skills and keen eye for design.

My applications are constructed with user experience in mind. I consider design elements that programmers might overlook such as site flow, brand colors, contrast, and typography. My designs are created with an idea of how the code will be written and pieced together.

I'm a proud ambivert - I possess both introvert and extrovert qualities. I like working independently but also enjoy helping others and excel at teamwork. I've been lucky enough to lead and coordinate groups as a supervisor for previous work, as a leader for school projects, and as a tech lead for a capstone project.

I spend my free time walking my dog, editing videos, and updating spreadsheets to organize my life. My interests include video games, puzzles, trail hiking, singing, and dancing.
